# Disclaimer

**Not legal advice. No claims of litigation outcome prediction.**

Refract Labs provides public-record revision chronology as an experimental research tool. It does not:

- Constitute legal advice or legal representation
- Predict or guarantee litigation outcomes
- Verify the truth, accuracy, or admissibility of any public record
- Replace the judgment of a qualified legal professional

The revision data Refract surfaces is drawn from publicly available MediaWiki histories. Refract does not interpret, assess, or editorialize about the content of those revisions.

If you are using Refract in connection with legal matters, you should:

1. Verify all revision data against the original wiki history
2. Consult with a qualified legal professional about admissibility
3. Understand that wiki content can change between observation and preservation

This is an experimental probe. Nothing here is a product or service for legal use.
